Multi-scenario underwriter. Base runs your inputs after state overlay (FL hurricane premium, TX property-tax shock, CA rent-cap drag, rust-belt rehab overrun). Stress layers realistic adverse conditions: rent −10%, vacancy +3pp, rate +100bps, insurance +20%, rehab +20%. Worst layers 90th-percentile-bad: rent −15%, vacancy +7pp, rate +200bps, insurance +40%, rehab +35%, exit cap +100bps. Gate: GREEN survives worst-case. YELLOW survives stress. RED breaks. price_to_green is the ceiling you'd have to negotiate to before the deal upgrades to GREEN — your walk-away number.
